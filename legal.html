GDPR Statement

University staff-student liaison committee is committed to ensuring the security and protection of the personal information that we process, and to provide a compliant and consistent approach to data protection. If you have any questions related to our GDPR compliance, please contact us.

We process your information to provide and improve our services, and to comply with law.

We are not using cookies on this website. Any of your information provided will be sent to us via secure protocal. We will take all reasonable steps to ensure that your information is kept secure. However, messages sent by email cannot be guaranteed to be completely secure as they may be intercepted, lost or corrupted
