What we do

The staff-student liaison committee is the name for a meeting where members of staff and student representatives discuss any academic issues that students have brought up to them and how to best solve these issues. The main issues that will be discussed are issues that involve either the Computer Science faculty, e.g. factual errors on a powerpoint, or the University as a whole, e.g. computer usage.

However, we unfortunately cannot discuss individual concerns, for example, if you might need some extra support in your studies. We also cannot discuss concerns about specific tutors or students — in these cases, you should report the situation to the University's Report and Support website, where you can report anonymously, or choose to include your personal details in the report.
